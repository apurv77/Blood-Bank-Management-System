About US

Our Mission

LifeShare's mission is to be the steward of a community resource; life-saving blood. LifeShare collects blood from the community to return it to the community at the lowest possible cost, consistent with the highest standards of quality.

LifeShare is an independent, locally-managed, non-profit community blood center. We are the primary blood supplier to the region's patients and hospitals.

We're members of India's Blood Centers, a group of 67 independent blood centers that supplies 50 percent of the country's blood. We subscribe to a community-based blood banking philosophy. That means community donors know that the blood they give stays in our community first -- helping family members, friends and neighbors. Excess supply is shared with other communities when needed.

Our vision is to build and strengthen the bonds of our community.
